ACIIDS 2016

About

ACIIDS 2016 is an international scientific conference for research in the field of intelligent information and database systems, to be held during 14-16 March, 2016 in Da Nang, Vietnam.

The aim of the conference is to provide an internationally respected forum for scientific research in the technologies and applications of intelligent information and database systems.

The conference is hosted by Vietnam-Korea Friendship Information Technology College (Viethanit), Vietnam and jointly organized by Wrocław University of Technology, Poland in cooperation with IEEE SMC Technical Committee on Computational Collective Intelligence, Bina Nusantara University, Indonesia, Ton DucThang University, Vietnam, and QuangBinh University, Vietnam.

The proceedings of ACIIDS 2016 will be published by Springer in series Lecture Notes in Artificial Intelligence LNCS/LNAI (confirmed).
