ACIIDS 2016

About
  • Date: 14-16 March 2016
  • Venue: Pullman Danang Beach Resort, Danang City, Vietnam
  • Official Website: https://aciids.pwr.edu.pl/

ACIIDS 2016 is an international scientific conference for research in the field of intelligent information and database systems, to be held during 14-16 March, 2016 in Da Nang, Vietnam.

The aim of the conference is to provide an internationally respected forum for scientific research in the technologies and applications of intelligent information and database systems.

The conference is hosted by Vietnam-Korea Friendship Information Technology College (Viethanit), Vietnam and jointly organized by Wroc≈Çaw University of Technology, Poland in cooperation with IEEE SMC Technical Committee on Computational Collective Intelligence, Bina Nusantara University, Indonesia, Ton DucThang University, Vietnam, and QuangBinh University, Vietnam.

The proceedings of ACIIDS 2016 will be published by Springer in series Lecture Notes in Artificial Intelligence LNCS/LNAI.

ViethanIT

Vietnam-Korea Friendship Information Technology College is the foundation of ICT training whose quality keeps pace with 3-year system of other countries in the regional and international area. This is a place to meet the requirement of ICT human resource for the Central and Highland region of Vietnam; satisfy the goals of industrialization and modernization of the country. It is going to be a Centre of Research and Technology Transfer of this region of Vietnam in the near future.
